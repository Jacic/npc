Welcome, my dear friend.

This is the official site of #NoPotatoCompo, a game jam created by @michcioperz and @Jacic (michcioperz and IamJacic on Twitter) as part of the #MeatUprising which happened when half of the #LD48 theme voters began to think 'Potato' would make a good theme.

Game Jam Rules

  1. Make a game in 48 hours.
  2. No potatoes in game.
  3. At least one potato in game's name.
  4. We challenge you to add some meat to your game. In case you consider meat itself not enough for your "dinner", rice is our featured potatoes replacement.
  5. Feeling a bit more creative? Add a paradox or two to the game, or at least a mention of a paradox.

If these rules are too simple for you, add Ludum Dare rules.

Communication during the Jam

We registered #NPC at irc.afternet.org. If you have any better ideas, add them to the issue tracker of this repo.

Submissions

@michcioperz is busy working on a WordPress-based submissions system. It should be ready before Friday, or even earlier.

Good luck, and have fun!
